Nightlife Is Broken - And Dangerous

Safety Crisis

68% of young adults feel unsafe. 1 in 5 women feel threatened. No unified safety tools—people rely on texts and hope.

Revenue Leakage

Venues miss VIP bookings and tips. Promoters lack efficient ticketing. No digital monetization infrastructure for experiences.

Fragmented Discovery

Users bounce between Google Maps, Yelp, and Instagram. No single source of truth for "what's happening tonight."

Trust Deficit

No way to verify venue safety or staff credentials. Bad actors go unchecked. Communities lack tools to hold venues accountable.
